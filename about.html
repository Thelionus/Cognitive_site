The Pedigree

Born from Deep Research

The Cognitive Group, headquartered in Montreal, was founded in 1995 as a specialized spinoff of the Computer Research Institute of Montreal (CRIM).

Leveraging decades of proprietary cognitive analysis capturing expert reasoning not present in public datasets or large language models, we partnered with the Royal Bank of Canada for over a decade to optimize high-stakes loan origination. This institutional discipline is embedded in the core of our deterministic AI engine.

Strategic Scale

Our alliance with CGI allows us to combine the agility of a deep-tech lab with the global delivery assurance required by Tier-1 institutions.
